Privacy Policy for ssssampler

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

SIMPLE VERSION

ssssampler doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Period.

THE DETAILS

Who We Are

ssssampler is developed by Nata Pokrovskaya. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

What Data We Collect

None. ssssampler does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

What Permissions We Need

Camera Access

The app needs access to your iPhone's camera to capture photos. All photo processing happens entirely on your device. We never see, store, or transmit your photos.

Photos Library Access

The app needs permission to save your composite images to your Photos library. You control which photos the app can access. All saving happens locally on your device. We never upload or transmit your photos anywhere.

In-App Purchases

ssssampler offers in-app purchases (base app €2.99, premium "prrrro" pack €4.99). All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing and may collect purchase data according to their own privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

ssssampler does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. No data is shared with third parties.

Data Storage

All photos you capture remain on your device. The app does not upload, sync, or back up your photos to any cloud service or server. Your photos are yours alone.

Children's Privacy

ssssampler does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data, there's nothing for us to delete, export, or modify. Simply uninstalling the app removes all app-related data from your device. Your photos remain in your Photos library and can be deleted through the Photos app.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle data (for example, if we add optional cloud features in the future), we will update this policy and notify users through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact: hello@ssssampler.com

App Store Compliance

This privacy policy complies with Apple App Store guidelines and applicable privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.
